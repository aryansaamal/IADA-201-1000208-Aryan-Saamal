# **MindPatch ‚Äì AI-Assisted Digital Detox & Wellness Planner üåø**

MindPatch is a **user-centred, AI-assisted digital wellness application prototype** designed to help students and young adults manage screen time, reduce digital fatigue, and maintain emotional balance.
The app focuses on **mindful technology use, emotional awareness, and positive habit formation** through gentle nudges rather than strict restrictions.

This project was developed as part of the **Critical and Creative Thinking ‚Äì Summative Assessment** under the **Artificial Intelligence course**.

---

## üìå **Project Purpose**

In today‚Äôs highly connected digital environment, students often experience:

* Excessive screen time
* Mental fatigue and anxiety
* Burnout due to constant digital engagement

MindPatch addresses these challenges by combining:

* Mood awareness
* Screen-time reflection
* AI-based detox planning
* Habit tracking with rewards

The core goal is to **support balance, not enforce control**.

---

## üîç **Main Research Findings**

Secondary research indicates that excessive screen time is strongly linked to increased stress, anxiety, and reduced emotional well-being among students. Studies emphasize that **self-awareness, gentle nudging, and habit-based interventions** are more effective than restrictive digital controls.

Research insights from existing wellness and productivity applications such as **Headspace** and **Forest** informed the design decisions. Their use of calm interfaces, motivation techniques, and non-judgmental behavior nudges influenced the emotional design, AI logic simulation, and overall user experience of MindPatch.

## üé® **Design Approach**

* Soft pastel color palette (lavender, light blue, white)
* Rounded UI components

## ü§ñ **AI Logic Simulation**

The AI behavior is **rule-based and simulated**, not predictive or data-harvesting.

### Example Logic:

```
IF mood < 3 AND screen time > 4 hours
‚Üí Suggest a detox break

IF habit streak is consistent
‚Üí Encourage continuation with motivation

IF late-night usage detected
‚Üí Suggest screen-free activity or sleep reminder
```

Over time, the system adapts conceptually to:

* Mood trends
* Time-of-day usage
* Habit consistency

This demonstrates **ethical AI thinking without implementing live AI models**.
